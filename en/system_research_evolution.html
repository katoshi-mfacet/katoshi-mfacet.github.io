Evolution Capability of System

Introduction

The main theme of my personal research, the origin of life, is based on the idea that chemical substances evolve in their natural state.

However, evolution usually refers to the evolution of biological species, and in a slightly broader sense, it is a concept used for things created by humans, such as technology and society.

In order to think about the evolution of chemical substances, we believe that it is important to use an approach that organizes and considers common concepts and differences, taking cues from the mechanisms of biological evolution and the evolution of things created by humans. I am.

Here, we will mainly organize the concepts of evolution that can be commonly applied to the evolution of chemical substances, the evolution of living things, and the evolution of things created by humans.
