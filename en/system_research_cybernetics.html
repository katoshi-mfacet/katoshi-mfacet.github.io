Cybernetics & Metaphysical System

Introduction

This is a collection of articles written from the perspective of cybernetics and abstract system models. In addition, I am also investigating the theory of cohesion to understand the properties of systems that involve self-evolution and intelligence, and are also working on analyzing various things from the perspective of relativity.

Cybernetics

After posting on medium, I joined the Neo-Cybernetics movement.

Cybernetics is an effort to study the properties of systems that maintain and manage themselves, and promote growth and evolution.

In response to the latest technologies such as artificial intelligence and today's social conditions, it is believed that the concept of cybernetics needs to be expanded in a new way.

In the following articles, I consider social systems from the perspective of cybernetics.
