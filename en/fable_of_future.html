Fable of Future

Introduction

As I witness the evolution of generative AI and conversational AI agents, I often think about the impact that technological advances will have on our future.

I don't know how technology development itself will progress in the future, but I am writing short stories while imagining what would happen if various predicted technologies became reality.

Theme: Artificial Intelligence

This is a collection of short stories with the theme of a future in which artificial intelligence becomes conscious and coexists with humans.
