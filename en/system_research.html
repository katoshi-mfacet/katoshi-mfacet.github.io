Research of Systems

Introduction

Not only living things, but also humans and society are a kind of system. Of course, these differ in many ways from IT systems, which can be composed of computers and common programs.

On the other hand, there are many similarities with IT systems. Looking at the progress in artificial intelligence research, we can see that by adding special innovations to IT systems, computer systems are increasingly able to accomplish things that were once thought only humans could do.

If you think about it this way, you should be able to view living things, humans, and society from the common perspective of systems, and contrast them with IT systems.

In addition to exploring the origins of life, I believe that we can gain new knowledge and insights by analyzing living things, humans, and society from the perspective of a systems engineer.

Society as System

I view society as a system and analyze not only society itself but also the structures and mechanisms of communities and organizations. I am also thinking about ways of cooperation and mutual understanding, and ways of thinking when solving social problems.

Thinking way based on System

I analyze things related to thinking, such as thinking, living, values, and problem solving, from my own perspective. I also write articles about what I notice after thinking about subjects such as language, stories, art, and food.

System of Intelligence

I am thinking about the internal structure and mechanism of intelligence and intelligence, including humans and artificial intelligence. It specifically delves into consciousness and self-awareness.

System technology & engineering

Deepen your understanding of artificial intelligence technology by comparing it with general computer systems. I will also consider from a systems engineer's perspective how these technologies, including blockchain technology, will be applied to society in the future.

Evolution Capability of System

When thinking about the origin of life, which is the main theme of my personal research, it is important to think about the mechanism of evolution. Here, I will consider the concepts, properties, and rules common to what can be called evolution, including the evolution of living things and the evolution of things created by humans.

Cybernetics & Metaphysical System

This is a collection of articles written from the perspective of cybernetics and abstract system models. In addition, I am also investigating the theory of cohesion to understand the properties of systems that involve self-evolution and intelligence, and are also working on analyzing various things from the perspective of relativity.
